## About

I am a first-year **Ph.D. candidate in Economics** at the [**University of Oslo**](https://www.sv.uio.no/econ/english/), advised by [Ragnhild C. Schreiner](https://sites.google.com/site/ragnhildschreiner/) and [Edwin Leuven](https://leuven.economists.nl/). My research focuses on **labor and public economics**, with secondary interests in **health economics and the economics of education**.

Prior to starting my Ph.D., I earned a Master's degree in Economics from the University of Mannheim and worked as a predoctoral researcher at Paris Dauphine University – PSL and the London Business School. 

You can find my full CV [here](cv.pdf).

I will be visiting **CREST** in June/July 2026, hosted by [Clément Malgouyres](https://sites.google.com/site/clementmalgouyres/).
